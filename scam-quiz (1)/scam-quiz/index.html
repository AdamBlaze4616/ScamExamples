Scam or Real?

A series of real-looking messages. Some are genuine, some are scams. Can you tell which is which?

Scammers send messages that look like they're from your bank, the government, a delivery company, a streaming service, or even a loved one. This quiz shows you examples so you can practise spotting the difference safely.

For each message you'll:

  • Look at it as if it arrived on your phone or in your inbox
  • Decide if it's a scam or a real message
  • See the answer and the warning signs to look for

These messages are made up for learning. None of the links, phone numbers or fees are real — please don't tap or call anything like them.
